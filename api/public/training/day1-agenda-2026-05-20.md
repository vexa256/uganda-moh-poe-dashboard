# Uganda POE Health Screening — Day 1 Training Agenda

**Date:** Tuesday, 2026-05-20  ·  **Time:** 11:00 – 16:00 EAT
**Build in the room:** Training Uganda POE Screening (Android, signed APK).
Detail, drills, scripts and glossary live in the Trainer's Manual.

| Time | Session | In one line | Class practical test |
|---|---|---|---|
| 11:00 – 11:30 | Pre-flight | Install the Training APK, sign in, confirm green sync. | **Green-sync salute** — every trainee holds up their phone showing name, role badge and a green sync indicator at the same time. No green, no progress. |
| 11:30 – 12:00 | Orientation | Roles, scope, menu, language — your role decides what you see. | **Partner menu read** — without touching your partner's phone, point at three menu items they can use and one they cannot, and explain why. Swap and repeat. |
| 12:00 – 12:45 | Primary Screening | Three hands-on traveller captures end-to-end. | **Three-capture sprint** — capture Travellers B, C and D within the block. Final Today counter must read **3**, Pending must read **0**, Referral Queue must read **1** (from C). Trainer eyeballs each phone. |
| 12:45 – 13:00 | Referral Queue | Open your first secondary case. | **Name the four** — open the referral you yourself created from Traveller C, then recite the four step names (Demographics → Vitals → Actions & Evidence → Disposition) aloud to your partner before standing up for lunch. |
| 13:00 – 14:00 | **Lunch** | | |
| 14:00 – 14:45 | Secondary Screening | Walk all four steps from Demographics to Close. | **Race to Close** — first five trainees to show a CLOSED case in Secondary Records with the correct outcome win the daily sticker. Trainer cross-checks the alert appears in Active Alerts for SUSPECTED outcomes. |
| 14:45 – 15:15 | Sync & In-App Reports | Offline drill and where your reports live in the app. | **Airplane-mode duel, in pairs** — one trainee captures with airplane mode on while the partner watches the indicator turn amber, then return to green when airplane mode is off. Then swap. Each pair states aloud the day's total from Primary Screening Report. |
| 15:15 – 15:45 | Web Dashboard | Guided tour, with a parallel admin / supervisor breakout. | **Find-the-fact race** — trainer calls three questions: today's screening volume at your POE, today's SUSPECTED alert count, your own sign-in time on the auth log. First hand up with the right answer earns a dashboard champion sticker. Admin breakout: each admin creates one test user and shows it in the user list. |
| 15:45 – 16:00 | Round-table & Sign-off | Q&A and a six-criterion can-do sign-off card. | **Six-tick sign-off** — tick Yes or No against six can-do statements on the sign-off card. Any No → 15-minute remedial immediately after. |
